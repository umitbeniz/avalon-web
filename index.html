Characters in the World of Avalon

Miner


-Miners extract ore from the mine and sell them.

-Miners extract iron, bronze, silver and gold from the mine based on the character's experience.

-While a miner can only mine iron at the beginning of the game, he will be able to mine more valuable ores as he gains experience.

Blacksmith


-Blacksmiths buy these ores and turn them into products.

-While blacksmiths produce standard swords at the beginning of the game, they will produce more powerful swords as they gain experience.

Warrior


-Warriors buy products crafted by blacksmiths and use them in battle.

-Warriors will gain $AVL faster with experience points gained by going into battle.
